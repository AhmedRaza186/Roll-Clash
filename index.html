üé≤ Game Rules: Roll Clash

This is a turn-based dice game based on risk-taking and chance.

üéØ Objective

  • Be the first player to reach a Total Score of 100 or more.

üìù Detailed Instructions for Players

1. Starting Your Turn

  1. Your Current Score for the turn starts at zero (0).
  2. Roll the Dice.

2. Roll Outcomes and Choices

  • If you roll a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6:
    • The number is added to your Current Score.
    • You must choose one of two options:
      • Roll Again: Click the Roll button to try for more points, risking your Current Score.
      • Hold Score: Click the Hold button to save your points (proceed to Step 3).
  • If you roll a 1 :
    • Your Current Score is immediately reset to zero (0).
    • You receive no points for this turn.
    • Your turn **immediately ends**, and play passes to the next player.

3. Holding and Saving Points

  • When you choose "Hold":
    • Your Current Score is added to your permanent Total Score.
    • The game checks if your Total Score is $\ge 100$.

4. Winning the Game

  • If your Total Score is 100 or higher after holding, You Win the Game!

5. Game Reset

  • If the game is reset, all players' Total Scores will be set to zero, and Player 1 will start.
